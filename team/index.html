Our Team

Interdisciplinary by necessity. Disciplined by choice.

Our team is interdisciplinary by necessity and disciplined by choice.

We bring together expertise in:

Celestial mechanics and space science

Scientific computing and numerical systems

Timekeeping, reference frames, and standards-aware engineering

Data integrity, documentation, and long-term system design

We value depth over titles, clarity over volume, and responsibility over visibility.

Every contributor understands both how a system works and what depends on it.
